Pollution & UV Index Over Time

Health Perspective

While Pollution affects your respiratory system, the UV Index tracks skin-damaging radiation. High levels of both simultaneously create "oxidative stress," making it especially important to stay hydrated and use skin protection.

Understanding the Scale

Pollution is measured via the Air Quality Index (AQI). A UV Index above 6 requires sun protection, even on cool or cloudy days, as clouds often do not block the harmful UVA/UVB rays shown in the trends above.

Traffic Data - Antwerp

Why this matters

Exposure to high pollution levels during peak traffic hours can increase respiratory strain by up to 30% during outdoor exercise.
